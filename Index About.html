About Us

Our Story

Flower Shop began its journey on March 26, 2025, blossoming from a simple dream into a vibrant haven for flower lovers in Bagasbas City. Our founder, Kristina Cassie E. Bagasbas, was inspired by her passion for nature’s beauty and the joy that flowers bring to every occasion. At just 19 years old, Kristina turned her love for blooms into a business dedicated to spreading happiness, comfort, and inspiration through exquisite floral arrangements.

What Makes Us Special

At Flower Shop, we believe every bouquet tells a unique story. Our arrangements are crafted with care, creativity, and a personal touch that sets us apart. We source only the freshest flowers and combine them in ways that reflect both tradition and modern elegance. Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and heartfelt service makes us the preferred choice for all your floral needs.

Our Services

  • Custom flower arrangements for all occasions
  • Same-day delivery within Bagasbas City
  • Event floral design (weddings, birthdays, corporate events)
  • Gift bouquets and plant baskets
  • Personalized floral consultations

Why Choose Flower Shop?

We go beyond selling flowers—we create memorable experiences. Our team is passionate, friendly, and always ready to help you find the perfect arrangement. With attention to detail, creative designs, and reliable service, we ensure every customer leaves with a smile. Our shop stands out for its dedication to quality, affordability, and a genuine love for what we do.
